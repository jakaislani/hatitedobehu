The Four season 1 contestant Elanese Lansen Bio, Boyfriend, Parents, Net Worth.

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, July 20, 2024

Elanese Lansen is an American arising singer whose prominence rises after competing in Fox's singing competition show, The Four: Battle for Stardom (season 1). Lansen is also popular for uploading cover songs on her official YouTube channel. 

The adorable passionate singer Elanese is working hard to pursue a better career in the music industry. It seems like she will change her passion into a profession soon. But, what about her personal life? Is Lansen having a romantic affair or living a single life at the moment? Know about it in the following sections. 

Early Life

Elanese was born on April 17, 1995, in Boca Raton, Florida, USA, under the zodiac sign of Aries. She is the daughter of Dominican-Puerto Rican parents Denise Sanchez (mother) and Joseph Sanchez (father). Elanese has a sister named Jonisse Sanchez.

Concerning education, Lansen graduated from Piper High School. She also went on to study at Olympic Heights Community High School. Later, in December 2020, Lansen graduated from the Florida Atlantic University (FAU), Florida, USA.

The 25-years-old Elanese has not revealed her present relationship status towards the media. Lansen has kept her personal life details far away from the limelight.

The lady has not shared any romantic pictures on her social networking sites. So, it seems like Elanese is living a single life without having any romantic affair. 

At the moment, Elanese is more focused to pursue a better career. Similarly, more than spending time dating someone she prefers to spend quality time with her friends and family members. Additionally, Elanese might also be searching for the right person to start her love life.

Contestants Of The Four (Season 1)

As shortly mentioned above, Elanese is one of the 15 contestants who participated in season 1 of The Four: Battle For Stardom. The show was released from January 4, 2018, to February 8, 2018.

The judges of the show were DJ Khaled, Charlie Walk, Sean Combs, and Meghan Trainor. In the first episode, Elanese sang the song 'Echame La Culpa,' by Demi Lovato, Luis Fonsi. 

Unfortunately, Lansen was eliminated at the end of episode 1 after losing the challenge round with another participant Zhavia Ward.

The winner of season 1 of The Four: Battle for Stardom is Evvie McKinney. Furthermore, The Four's season 1 was premiered on June 7, 2018, and ended on August 2, 2018. The winner of The Four season 2 is James Graham.
