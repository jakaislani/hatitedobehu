AEW Double Or Nothing 2022 Betting Odds Currently Favor New Champions Being Crowned

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, July 4, 2024

AEW will present its fourth Double or Nothing event this Sunday, May 29, 2022. The event returns to Los Angeles for the first time since the inaugural AEW event in 2019 and in the main event, CM Punk will challenge for the AEW World Championship against Adam “Hangman” Page.

According to new betting odds from BetOnline.AG, CM Punk is currently favored to win the World Championship from Adam Page and if he does, it will be his first World Championship in nearly a decade as he lost the WWE Championship to the Rock at the 2013 Royal Rumble event and would never capture another World Title in pro wrestling.
