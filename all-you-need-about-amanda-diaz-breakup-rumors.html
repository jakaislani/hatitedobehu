All You Need About Amanda Diaz Breakup Rumors

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, September 7, 2024

When Amanda Diaz refers to her boyfriend Kio Cyr as her best friend, breakup rumors are stoked.

Amanda Diaz has been having a great time while on holiday in Ibiza, Spain, for the past few days. She posts a lot of bikini photographs on Instagram and TikTok videos because she is a popular content creator.

But on June 6, 2022, she posted a TikTok, which caused her to appear on tea channels and become the subject of breakup rumors.

She was given some bread in the video by someone whose hands were only seen. It was clear from the tattoos on the hand that it was not her boyfriend Kio Cyr.

Even one user claimed it wasn’t Cyr in the comments, to which Diaz responded, “I can have pals. LMAO.”

Another user urged the TikToker to discuss her relationship with her boyfriend in-depth, but instead of doing so, the TikToker simply asked the user to “respectfully” cease.

She Calls Kio Cyr, her boyfriend, her best friend

Snapshots of these exchanges between Diaz and the users were posted on Instagram by the original Tiktokroom.

In the comment area, online users expressed their conviction that Diaz and Cyr had split up.

One commenter remarked,

“Yeah, they ain’t together, why is another guy feeding her lol.” Another reader commented, “No guy save your man should be feeding you period.

Some users even believed that Diaz and Cyr had split up because they both likely cheated on each other, much like they had done with Olivia Ponton, Cyr’s ex-girlfriend.

However, numerous individuals backed her, urging viewers to refrain from responding to what might just be a lighthearted video.

Everyone is overreacting when she is on vacation with friends. They may both have pals and be loyal, one commenter who appeared to be on Diaz’s side remarked.

The user was correct, too. The tattooed hands belonged to Diaz’s companion Romane Royale, who is also on vacation with Diaz in Ibiza, according to internet sleuths.

Before more speculative comments could be made, Diaz moved to the comment area and wrote,

“Kio is my best buddy, and practically nothing has changed; I’m simply sick of people prying into my personal affairs. It becomes too much.

Relationship between Kio Cyr and Amanda Diaz

Despite Diaz’s silence on the subject, many online users thought that she and Cyr had broken up because she referred to him as her best friend rather than her boyfriend.

Girl, we know yall broke up, just say that one person remarked, while another questioned why she claimed he was her boyfriend rather than her closest friend.

Some online users, though, continued to believe that the couple was still together. They suggested that Diaz’s lover might double as her best friend. Since neither party has said anything, it is only rumored that they are divorcing.

Furthermore, Cyr has a photo of him and Diaz pinned on his Instagram, and they haven’t removed any images of one another from their accounts.

When the two first met, they were closest friends, Cyr said to Hollywire in June 2021. He claimed he wasn’t dating anyone and didn’t want to jeopardize his connection with Diaz.

However, it appeared as though he had altered his mind by December 2021. He and Diaz were dating and frequently shared affectionate photos on Instagram.
