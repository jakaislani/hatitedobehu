Michael Douglas Movies And TV Shows: Michael Douglas Films In Order

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, June 18, 2024

Michael Douglas is an American actor, politician, and film producer. As a politician, he is on the ticket of the Democratic Party.

Michael Douglas is acknowledged for his humanitarian and political activism. He is an honorary board member of the anti-war grant-making foundation Ploughshares Fund. The famous actor was appointed as a United Nations Messenger of Peace in 1998.

He stands at a height of 1.78m and weighs 70kg. Michael Douglas was born to his parents, Kirk Douglas, and Diana Douglas. He had his education at The Allen-Stevenson School, Eaglebrook School, The Choate Preparatory School, and the University of California, Santa Barbara.

Michael Douglas’s net worth of $350 million. His net worth is sourced from his career as a politician, actor, and film producer.
