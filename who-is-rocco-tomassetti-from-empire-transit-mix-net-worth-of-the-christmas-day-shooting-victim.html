Who Is Rocco Tomassetti From Empire Transit Mix? Net Worth Of The Christmas Day Shooting Victim

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, August 30, 2024

Rocco Tomassetti is a construction magnate who owns Empire Transit Mix. The company helped to shape the skyline in New York City.

Rocco Tomassetti and his wife, Vinceta Marsicano-Tomassettti, were shot inside their house on Christmas morning. The shooter has been identified as their son, Dino Tomassetti.

Police are still investigating the case and have not provided reasons why he shot his parents.

Who Is Rocco Tomassetti From Empire Transit Mix?

Rocco Tomassetti is the owner of Empire Transit Mix. It is a cement and concrete product manufacturing company in Brooklyn, New York. According to the D&B business directory, it was founded in 1992. The company has 40 employees and generates sales worth $5.31 million.

The Tomasetti family is descended from the late Dino Tomasetti Sr., an Italian immigrant whose Laquila building empire helped establish New York City’s skyline.

According to Daily Mail, the Goldman Sachs headquarters near Ground Zero, the Bank of America headquarters, apartments, and more are among their projects. His son, Rocco’s Empire Transit Mix, produced the concrete for the Freedom Tower, and his daughter’s firm assisted in its development.

Unfortunately, the 65 years old Rocco has died after his son, Dino Tomassetti Jr., shot him in the back on Christmas morning. He fled the scene in a Cadillac Escalade before being apprehended later in New Jersey.
