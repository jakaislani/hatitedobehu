Farmall Super A Tractor Serial Numbers, Price, Reviews, Specs, Engine Oil Capacity, Attachments, History, Features & Pictures

The Farmall Super A tractor was built by International Harvester from 1947 to 1954 in Illinois, Louisville and Chicago, Ke,ntucky, USA.

The IH Farmall Super A tractor was an improved version of the A. The most significant change was that the previous Lift-All system was restored with the new Touch Control hydraulic system. It was in response to the Ferguson 3-point system applied by Ford.
