Jeannie Mai | Net Worth and Income

Multi-talented fashion stylist and television host Jeannie Mai’s net worth is a staggering $4 million.

She has accumulated huge wealth through her career in the beauty and entertainment industry.

Jeannie Mai | Career

While residing in San Jose, Mai began her work as a cosmetic artist for MAC Cosmetics at 18.

She gained experience as a trainer by working her way up from face to face till she was finally traveling the globe to work for celebrities like Alicia Keys and Christina Aguilera.

Her work as a celebrity makeup artist led to her working for MTV’s Total Request Live, Good Day Sacramento, and KCAL Los Angeles.

In order to showcase her presenting skills, Mai started submitting self-written scripts to local television networks in 2003.

She was chosen to co-host the International Channel’s Asian-American magazine-style program “Stir.”

Later, she was hired to host her own music countdown by the San Francisco-based California Music Channel.

Mai went on to work as a producer for the WB’s The Daily Mixx and an entertainment reporter.

In 2005, Jeannie was hired as the host of “Character Fantasy” on the USA Network, marking her first primetime hosting gig.
