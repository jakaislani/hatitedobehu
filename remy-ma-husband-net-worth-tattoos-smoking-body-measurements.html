On 30-5-1980 Remy Ma was born in New York City, New York. She made her 4 million dollar fortune with There’s Something About Remy: Based on a True Story, Love & Hip Hop: New York & All the Way Up. The tv-personality & musician is married to Papoose, her starsign is Gemini and she is now 42 years of age.

Remy Ma is a rapper who started her career when she was discovered by Big Pun but her big break came when she joined Fat Joe’s group called Terror Squad. There have been only 5 female rappers who made it to the Billboard Hot 100 chart – and she is one of them.

She has been married to Papoose since 2016 and they appeared together on VH1’s reality series Love & Hip Hop: New York. They were scheduled to marry in 2008 but Remy Ma was in prison at the time after being convicted of illegal weapon possession, assault, and attempted coercion in connection with these charges. She spent 6 years in jail and was released on August 1, 2014.

Remy Ma gave birth to her first child in 2000, a son she shares with her ex-boyfriend, and she has 3 step-children with her husband.
