Red White and Blue Rice Krispies Treats Bark

These sweet treats are sure to satisfy everyone! Colorful and festive, this Red White and Blue Rice Krispies Treats Bark is the perfect no-bake treat to celebrate with!

Course Dessert Cuisine American Keyword red white and blue rice krispies treats bark, rice krispies, Rice Krispies Treats Prep Time 5 minutes minutes Total Time 5 minutes minutes Servings 24 servings Calories 255kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

Ingredients

  • 10 oz miniature marshmallows
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 5 cup Rice Krispies cereal
  • 3 oz JELL-O 1 box - you pick the flavor!
  • 16 oz vanilla flavored candy coating I used CandiQuik
  • 2 cups red white and blue candies and sprinkles I used jimmies, red vines, m&ms

Instructions

  • Line a 10x15 jellyroll pan with parchment paper

  • Combine marshmallows and one quarter cup of butter in a microwave-safe bowl. Cover and heat on high for 30 seconds, stir. Repeat until completely blended.

  • Stir in the JELL-O until completely combined.

  • Stir in cereal and continue mixing until cereal is completely coated with marshmallow mixture.

  • Press mixture into prepared pan firmly. Use a small piece of parchment paper to keep the treats from sticking to your hands.

  • Let sit for 10 minutes.

  • Melt candy coating according to package directions and spread on top of the rice krispies treats.

  • Quickly sprinkle with candies.

  • Let the bark set up in the fridge for 15 minutes or on the counter for an hour before gently breaking apart.
